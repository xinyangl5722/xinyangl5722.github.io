SEP 10 Freedom Project

Context

For this project, I had to make a website to show how technology can be corporated to a certain topic we chose. This project uses all of the content of coding we learned from the past year such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, etc. and we incorporated all the content into a website.

Content

This project shows how technology fuses with art and animation. First, it shows the context of art and animation and how it has evolved. Then, it gave examples of softwares of art and animation like Ibis Paint X and Flipaclip, and hardwares like a stylus and a tablet. Last but not least, it shows my own future innovations such as an app that fuses drawing and animation together and a stylus that can change brushes and colors.

Reflection

Overall, this project helped me with a ton of skills such as researching from researching softwares and hardwares, organization and planning which helps my website becoming orderly and planned neatly, and creativity from thinking about future innovations.

One of my challenges has to be doing my Aframe which is my model of the stylus in my website. It was pretty difficult to move the small cylinders which are the buttons and screens to where I want them to be, but I continued to move around with the cylinders and it worked out a few minutes later.

If I could change the website, I would probably make my website less poorly made and make it into more of an actual functioning website that actually looks pleasing and looks functioning to use.
